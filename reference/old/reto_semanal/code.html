Challenge of the Week:
The 10-Minute Check-in

A ritual to align on the upcoming week and express needs. This simple practice ensures both partners feel seen and supported before the Monday rush begins.

verified Reduces Sunday-evening anxiety
schedule 10 Minutes

How to do it

1

Find a Quiet Spot

Choose a comfortable space without digital distractions. Put phones on Do Not Disturb.

2

Calendar Review

Briefly look at the next 7 days. Highlight busy evenings or stressful appointments.

3

Share the One Thing

Each partner answers: "What is one thing I can do this week to make you feel loved?"

Your Goal

Complete 3 times this week

2/3 Done
